# Chicken Tikka Masala

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup cream
- Salt

## Steps
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, garam masala, cumin, paprika, and salt for 30 minutes.
2. Sear chicken in oil until browned; set aside.
3. Saute onion until soft. Add garlic and ginger; cook 1 minute.
4. Add tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and return chicken to the pan.
6. Simmer 8-10 minutes until chicken is cooked through. Serve with rice.
