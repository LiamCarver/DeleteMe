Mushroom Risotto

Servings: 4
Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients
- 4 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth, warmed
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 shallot, finely diced
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- Salt and pepper
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley

Steps
1. Heat butter and olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add shallot and cook until soft, then add mushrooms and cook until browned.
3. Stir in rice and cook for 1 minute to toast.
4. Pour in wine and stir until absorbed.
5. Add warm broth 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until absorbed before adding more.
6. Continue until rice is creamy and tender, 18 to 20 minutes.
7. Stir in Parmesan, season, and finish with parsley.
