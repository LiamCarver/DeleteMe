Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups arborio rice
- 4 cups warm vegetable stock
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 250 g mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- Salt and black pepper

Steps:
1. Saute onion in oil and 1 tbsp butter until soft; add mushrooms and cook until browned.
2. Stir in rice and toast for 1 minute.
3. Add wine and cook until absorbed.
4. Add stock 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until absorbed before adding more.
5. Cook until rice is creamy and tender, about 18 to 20 minutes.
6. Stir in remaining butter, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
