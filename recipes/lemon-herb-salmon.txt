Lemon Herb Salmon

Ingredients:
- 4 salmon fillets
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tsp chopped dill
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- Salt and black pepper

Steps:
1. Heat oven to 200 C.
2. Place salmon on a baking sheet and brush with olive oil.
3. Top with garlic, herbs, lemon slices, salt, and pepper.
4. Bake 12 to 15 minutes until flaky.
5. Serve with extra lemon.
