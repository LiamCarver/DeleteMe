Lemon Herb Salmon

Servings: 4
Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients
- 4 salmon fillets
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried parsley
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- Salt and pepper

Steps
1. Heat oven to 400 F. Pat salmon dry and season with salt and pepper.
2. Melt butter with olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
3. Add garlic, lemon juice, zest, parsley, and thyme. Stir for 30 seconds.
4. Add salmon, spoon sauce over the top, and transfer skillet to oven.
5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
