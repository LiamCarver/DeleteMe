Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 can (400 g) diced tomatoes
- 2 cans (400 g) chickpeas, drained
- 1 cup coconut milk
- Salt
- Fresh cilantro

Steps:
1. Heat oil and saute onion until soft; add garlic and ginger for 1 minute.
2. Stir in curry powder and cumin for 30 seconds.
3. Add tomatoes and chickpeas; simmer 10 minutes.
4. Stir in coconut milk and cook 5 minutes.
5. Season with salt and top with cilantro.
