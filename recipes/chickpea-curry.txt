Chickpea Curry

Servings: 4
Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh cilantro, to serve

Steps
1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Cook onion until soft.
2. Add garlic and ginger for 30 seconds, then stir in curry powder, cumin, and turmeric.
3. Add tomatoes and simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Stir in chickpeas and coconut milk. Simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper and serve with cilantro.
