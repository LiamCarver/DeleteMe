Classic Caesar Salad

Servings: 4
Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients
- 1 large romaine heart, chopped
- 1/2 cup croutons
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 anchovy fillets, mashed (optional)
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Steps
1. Whisk garlic, anchovy, Dijon, lemon juice, and Worcestershire.
2. Slowly whisk in olive oil until emulsified. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Toss romaine with dressing, then add croutons and Parmesan.
4. Serve immediately.
