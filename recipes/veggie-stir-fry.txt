Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 carrot, sliced thin
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps:
1. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and water.
2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet.
3. Add garlic and ginger; cook 30 seconds.
4. Add vegetables and stir-fry 4 to 5 minutes.
5. Pour in sauce and cook until glossy.
6. Serve over rice or noodles.
