Spaghetti Carbonara

Serves: 4
Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 4 oz pancetta, diced
- 2 large eggs
- 1 large egg yolk
- 3/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano
- 1/2 tsp black pepper, plus more to taste
- 1 tsp kosher salt (for pasta water)

Steps:
1) Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2) While pasta cooks, saute pancetta in a skillet over medium heat until crisp. Remove from heat.
3) In a bowl, whisk eggs, yolk, cheese, and pepper until smooth.
4) Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain spaghetti.
5) Toss hot spaghetti with pancetta. Off heat, add egg mixture and toss quickly.
6) Add splashes of pasta water until glossy and creamy. Serve with extra cheese and pepper.
