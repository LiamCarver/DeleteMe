Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:
- 8 oz spaghetti
- 2 large eggs
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- 4 oz pancetta, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Black pepper
- Salt

Steps:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl; set aside.
3. Saute pancetta until crisp; add garlic for 30 seconds.
4. Toss drained pasta with pancetta off heat.
5. Stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed for a silky sauce.
6. Season with black pepper and salt to taste. Serve warm.
