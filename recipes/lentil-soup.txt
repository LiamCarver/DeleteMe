Lentil Soup

Ingredients:
- 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Saute onion, carrots, and celery in oil until softened.
2. Add garlic and cumin; cook 30 seconds.
3. Add lentils and broth; bring to a boil.
4. Simmer 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.
