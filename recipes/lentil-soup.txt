Lentil Soup

Ingredients:
- 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Heat olive oil in a pot and saute onion, carrots, celery.
2. Add garlic and cumin; cook 30 seconds.
3. Add lentils, broth, and tomatoes.
4. Simmer 25 to 30 minutes until lentils are tender.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
