Lentil Soup

Summary:
Hearty soup with lentils, vegetables, and warming spices.

Ingredients:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 1.5 cups dried lentils, rinsed
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Lemon wedges, for serving

Steps:
1) Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Saute onion, carrot, and celery until soft.
2) Add garlic, cumin, and coriander; cook 30 seconds.
3) Stir in lentils, broth, and bay leaf. Bring to a boil.
4) Reduce heat and simmer 25 to 30 minutes until lentils are tender.
5) Season with salt and pepper. Remove bay leaf.
6) Serve with a squeeze of lemon.
