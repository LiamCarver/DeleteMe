Greek Salad

Summary:
Fresh salad with cucumber, tomato, olives, feta, and a lemon-oregano dressing.

Ingredients:
- 2 cups chopped cucumber
- 2 cups chopped tomato
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
- 4 oz feta, cubed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:
1) Combine cucumber, tomato, onion, olives, and feta in a bowl.
2) Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.
3) Pour dressing over salad and toss gently.
4) Serve immediately.
