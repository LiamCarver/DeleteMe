Greek Salad

Serves: 4
Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:
- 3 cups chopped cucumber
- 2 cups chopped tomatoes
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives
- 4 oz feta, cubed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps:
1) Combine cucumber, tomatoes, onion, olives, and feta in a bowl.
2) Whisk olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
3) Toss dressing with salad and serve.
