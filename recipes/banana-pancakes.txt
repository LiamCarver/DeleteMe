Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- 1 ripe banana, mashed

Steps:
1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
2. Whisk milk, egg, butter, and banana in another bowl.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
4. Cook on a greased griddle over medium heat, 2-3 minutes per side.
5. Serve with syrup.
