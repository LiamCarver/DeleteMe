Banana Pancakes

Summary:
Fluffy pancakes with mashed banana and a hint of cinnamon.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 ripe banana, mashed
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps:
1) Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
2) In another bowl, mix banana, milk, egg, and butter.
3) Combine wet and dry ingredients just until mixed.
4) Cook 1/4-cup portions on a greased skillet over medium heat until bubbles form.
5) Flip and cook until golden. Serve warm.
