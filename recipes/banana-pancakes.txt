Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:
- 2 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- Oil or butter for the pan

Steps:
1. Whisk bananas, milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt; fold into wet ingredients.
3. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat.
4. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake; cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook through.
