Banana Pancakes

Serves: 3-4
Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 2 tbsp melted butter, plus more for cooking
- 2 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:
1) Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
2) Whisk milk, egg, melted butter, bananas, and vanilla in another bowl.
3) Fold wet into dry just until combined.
4) Cook 1/4-cup portions on a buttered skillet over medium heat until bubbles form.
5) Flip and cook until golden. Serve warm.
