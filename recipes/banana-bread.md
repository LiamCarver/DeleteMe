# Banana Bread

## Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Grease a loaf pan.
2. Mix bananas and melted butter in a bowl.
3. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
4. Mix in baking soda and salt, then flour.
5. Pour into pan and bake 50-60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
6. Cool before slicing.
