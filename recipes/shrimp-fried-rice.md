# Shrimp Fried Rice

## Ingredients
- 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 3 cups cooked rice, chilled
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Salt and pepper

## Steps
1. Scramble eggs in a hot pan with a little oil; set aside.
2. Stir-fry shrimp until pink; remove.
3. Add remaining oil and stir-fry peas and carrots.
4. Add rice and toss to heat through.
5. Return shrimp and eggs, add soy sauce and sesame oil.
6. Toss well, season, and top with green onions.
