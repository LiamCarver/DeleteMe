Beef Tacos

Serves: 4
Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, salsa

Steps:
1) Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until soft, then add garlic.
2) Add beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
3) Stir in spices, salt, pepper, and tomato sauce. Simmer 5 minutes.
4) Warm taco shells or tortillas.
5) Fill with beef and add toppings. Serve immediately.
