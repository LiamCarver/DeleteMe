Beef Tacos

Summary:
Seasoned ground beef served in warm tortillas with fresh toppings.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1/3 cup water
- 8 small tortillas
- Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomato, onion, cheese, salsa

Steps:
1) Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain excess fat.
2) Add spices, salt, pepper, and water. Simmer 5 minutes until thickened.
3) Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
4) Fill tortillas with beef and desired toppings.
5) Serve immediately.
