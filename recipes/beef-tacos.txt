Beef Tacos

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp taco seasoning
- 1/4 cup water
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomato, cheese, salsa

Steps:
1. Brown beef in a skillet; drain fat.
2. Stir in taco seasoning and water; simmer 3-4 minutes.
3. Warm taco shells or tortillas.
4. Fill with beef and desired toppings.
5. Serve immediately.
