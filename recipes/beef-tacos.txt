Beef Tacos

Servings: 4
Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- Shredded lettuce, diced tomato, and cheese, to serve

Steps
1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion until soft.
2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add ground beef and brown.
3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
4. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Fill taco shells with beef and top with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
