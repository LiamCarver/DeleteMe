Beef Tacos

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup water
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- Shredded lettuce
- Diced tomato
- Shredded cheese

Steps:
1. Brown beef in a skillet; drain fat.
2. Add spices and water; simmer 5 minutes.
3. Warm taco shells or tortillas.
4. Fill with beef and toppings.
