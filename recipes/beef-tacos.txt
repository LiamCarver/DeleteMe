Beef Tacos

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- Salt and pepper
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, salsa

Steps:
1. Brown ground beef in a skillet; drain excess fat.
2. Add spices, salt, and pepper with 1/4 cup water.
3. Simmer 5 minutes until thickened.
4. Warm taco shells or tortillas.
5. Fill with beef and toppings.
