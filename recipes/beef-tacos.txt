Beef Tacos

Ingredients:
- 500 g ground beef
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning
- 1/4 cup water
- 8 taco shells
- Shredded lettuce, diced tomato, cheese

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a skillet and cook onion until soft; add garlic for 30 seconds.
2. Add ground beef and brown until cooked through.
3. Stir in taco seasoning and water; simmer 3 minutes.
4. Warm taco shells.
5. Fill shells with beef and toppings.
