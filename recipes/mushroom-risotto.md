# Mushroom Risotto

## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
- 4 cups warm chicken or vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 1/2 cup white wine
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- Salt and pepper

## Steps
1. Saute mushrooms in olive oil until browned; set aside.
2. Melt butter and cook onion until soft.
3. Add rice and toast 1 minute.
4. Add wine and stir until absorbed.
5. Add broth 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until absorbed before adding more.
6. Stir in mushrooms and Parmesan; season to taste.
