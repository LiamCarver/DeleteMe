Shrimp Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked rice, chilled
- 8 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil, scramble eggs, and set aside.
2. Add remaining oil, cook shrimp until pink; remove.
3. Stir-fry peas and carrots for 2 minutes.
4. Add rice and stir-fry until hot.
5. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, eggs, and shrimp.
6. Toss with green onions and season to taste.
