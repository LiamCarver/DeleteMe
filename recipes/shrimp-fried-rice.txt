Shrimp Fried Rice

Summary:
Quick fried rice with shrimp, vegetables, and a light soy sauce seasoning.

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 cups cooked rice, chilled
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots
- 3 scallions, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps:
1) Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large skillet. Cook shrimp until pink; remove.
2) Add remaining oil, then scramble eggs; push to one side.
3) Add rice and peas and carrots. Stir-fry 2 to 3 minutes.
4) Return shrimp to the pan. Add soy sauce and sesame oil.
5) Toss with scallions and season to taste.
