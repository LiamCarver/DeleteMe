Pad Thai

Serves: 3-4
Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:
- 8 oz rice noodles
- 2 tbsp tamarind paste
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup cooked shrimp or chicken
- 1 cup bean sprouts
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
- Lime wedges, for serving

Steps:
1) Soak rice noodles in warm water until pliable; drain.
2) Whisk tamarind, fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice to make sauce.
3) Heat oil in a wok. Add garlic, then eggs; scramble.
4) Add noodles and sauce; toss until coated.
5) Add shrimp, bean sprouts, and green onions; stir-fry 2 minutes.
6) Serve with peanuts and lime wedges.
