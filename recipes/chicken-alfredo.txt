Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
- 12 oz fettuccine
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 3/4 cup grated parmesan
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps:
1. Cook fettuccine in salted water; drain.
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
3. Saute chicken in olive oil until cooked; remove.
4. Melt butter, add garlic for 30 seconds.
5. Stir in cream and simmer 3 minutes.
6. Add parmesan and stir until smooth.
7. Toss pasta and chicken with sauce.
