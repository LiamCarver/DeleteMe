Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell pepper, carrot)
- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a wok over high heat.
2. Add garlic and stir for 10 seconds.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until crisp-tender.
4. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
5. Stir in cornstarch slurry to thicken, 30 seconds.
6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
