Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 carrot, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger

Steps:
1. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch.
2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok; add garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender.
4. Pour in sauce and toss until glossy and thickened.
5. Serve over rice or noodles.
