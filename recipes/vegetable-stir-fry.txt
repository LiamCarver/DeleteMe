Vegetable Stir-Fry

Serves: 3-4
Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 carrot, thinly sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
- 1 tsp sesame oil

Steps:
1) Heat oil in a wok or skillet over high heat.
2) Add broccoli and carrot; stir-fry 2 minutes.
3) Add bell pepper and snap peas; stir-fry 2 minutes more.
4) Add garlic and ginger; cook 30 seconds.
5) Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, and honey. Add cornstarch slurry and simmer 1 minute.
6) Finish with sesame oil and serve over rice or noodles.
