Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 carrot, thinly sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a wok over high heat.
2. Add bell pepper, carrot, broccoli, and snap peas; stir-fry 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Add garlic and ginger; cook 30 seconds.
4. Stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
5. Add cornstarch slurry and cook until glossy.
6. Serve over steamed rice.
