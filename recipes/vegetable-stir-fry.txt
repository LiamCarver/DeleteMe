Vegetable Stir-Fry

Summary:
Crisp vegetables tossed in a savory sauce and served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 cup snap peas
- 1 carrot, thinly sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce (or extra soy sauce)
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Steps:
1) Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
2) Add broccoli and carrot; stir-fry 2 minutes.
3) Add bell pepper and snap peas; stir-fry 2 minutes more.
4) Add garlic and ginger; stir-fry 30 seconds.
5) Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
6) Add cornstarch slurry and cook until glossy, about 1 minute.
7) Serve hot over rice or noodles.
