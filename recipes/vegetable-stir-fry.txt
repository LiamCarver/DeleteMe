Vegetable Stir-Fry

Servings: 4
Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 carrot, thinly sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
- 2 green onions, sliced

Steps
1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
2. Add bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 4 to 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 30 seconds.
4. Stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey. Add cornstarch slurry and cook until glossy.
5. Finish with green onions and serve over rice.
