Miso Ramen

Serves: 2
Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:
- 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp white miso
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 packs ramen noodles (discard seasoning)
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved
- 2 scallions, sliced

Steps:
1) Bring broth to a simmer in a pot.
2) Whisk miso with a ladle of hot broth, then stir back into pot.
3) Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and mushrooms; simmer 5 minutes.
4) Cook noodles in the broth until tender. Add spinach to wilt.
5) Serve with eggs and scallions.
