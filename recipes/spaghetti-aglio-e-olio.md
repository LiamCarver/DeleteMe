# Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

## Ingredients
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup chopped parsley
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan (optional)
- Salt and black pepper

## Steps
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Warm olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
3. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook 1-2 minutes until fragrant, not browned.
4. Add drained spaghetti and a splash of pasta water. Toss to coat.
5. Season with salt and black pepper. Stir in parsley.
6. Serve with Parmesan if using.
