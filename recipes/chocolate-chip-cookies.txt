Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps:
1. Heat oven to 350 F and line a baking sheet.
2. Cream butter and sugars; beat in egg and vanilla.
3. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt, then fold in chips.
4. Scoop dough onto sheet and bake 10-12 minutes.
5. Cool on a rack before serving.
