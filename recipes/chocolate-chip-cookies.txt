Chocolate Chip Cookies

Summary:
Chewy cookies with crisp edges and plenty of chocolate chips.

Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps:
1) Preheat oven to 375 F and line baking sheets with parchment.
2) Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.
3) Cream butter and sugars until light. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
4) Mix in dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.
5) Scoop dough onto sheets and bake 9 to 11 minutes.
6) Cool on rack before serving.
