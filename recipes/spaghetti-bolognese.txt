Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 celery stalk, diced
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper
- Olive oil

Steps:
1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente; drain.
2. Saute onion, carrot, and celery in olive oil until soft.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
4. Brown ground beef; season with salt and pepper.
5. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, and basil.
6. Simmer 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
