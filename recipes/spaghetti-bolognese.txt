Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente; drain.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet, saute onion until soft.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
4. Brown beef, then drain excess fat.
5. Stir in tomato paste, tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, pepper.
6. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
7. Toss sauce with spaghetti and serve.
