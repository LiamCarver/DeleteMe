Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:
- 400 g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 500 g ground beef
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and black pepper
- Grated Parmesan

Steps:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Heat oil in a pan, saute onion until soft, then add garlic for 30 seconds.
3. Add ground beef and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
4. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper.
5. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes, loosening with pasta water if needed.
6. Toss sauce with spaghetti and serve with Parmesan.
