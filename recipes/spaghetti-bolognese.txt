Spaghetti Bolognese

Servings: 4
Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 celery stalk, diced
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper
- 12 oz spaghetti
- Grated Parmesan, to serve

Steps
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Saute onion, carrot, and celery until soft, 5 to 7 minutes.
2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Stir in ground beef and cook until browned.
3. Mix in tomato paste, then add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
4. Simmer the sauce for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
6. Toss spaghetti with sauce and serve with Parmesan.
