# Beef Tacos

## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- Toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese, salsa

## Steps
1. Cook onion in oil until soft.
2. Add ground beef and brown; drain excess fat.
3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
4. Warm taco shells or tortillas.
5. Fill with beef and desired toppings.
