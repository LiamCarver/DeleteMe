Margherita Pizza

Servings: 2 to 3
Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients
- 1 pizza dough ball (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 8 to 10 fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper

Steps
1. Heat oven to 475 F. If using a stone, preheat it for 20 minutes.
2. Mix crushed tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Stretch dough into a 12-inch round on a floured surface.
4. Spread sauce thinly, then add mozzarella slices.
5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
6. Top with basil leaves and slice.
