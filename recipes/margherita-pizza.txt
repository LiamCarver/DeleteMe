Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough ball
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 200 g fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 240 C and heat a pizza stone if you have one.
2. Stretch dough into a 12 inch round.
3. Spread tomato sauce thinly; add mozzarella and basil.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of salt.
5. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until crust is golden.
