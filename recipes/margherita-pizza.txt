Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

Steps:
1. Heat oven to 475 F and place a pizza stone if available.
2. Stretch dough into a round and place on a baking sheet.
3. Spread tomato sauce and add mozzarella.
4. Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
5. Top with basil, drizzle olive oil, and add a pinch of salt.
