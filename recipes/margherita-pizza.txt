Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough ball
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

Steps:
1. Heat oven to 475 F.
2. Stretch dough into a round on a baking sheet or stone.
3. Spread tomato sauce and add mozzarella.
4. Bake 10 to 12 minutes until crust is golden.
5. Top with basil, drizzle olive oil, and add a pinch of salt.
