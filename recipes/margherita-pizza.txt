Margherita Pizza

Summary:
Classic pizza with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil, and olive oil.

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil, plus more for drizzling
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 6 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves

Steps:
1) Preheat oven to 500 F (or highest setting) with a baking stone or sheet inside.
2) Mix tomatoes with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
3) Stretch dough into a 12-inch round on parchment.
4) Spread sauce, add mozzarella slices, and drizzle lightly with olive oil.
5) Bake 8 to 12 minutes until crust is blistered and cheese melts.
6) Top with basil leaves and serve.
