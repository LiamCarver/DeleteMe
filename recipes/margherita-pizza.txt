Margherita Pizza

Serves: 2-3
Time: 30 minutes (plus dough proofing if homemade)

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- Extra olive oil for drizzling

Steps:
1) Heat oven to 500F. Place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
2) Mix tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, and salt.
3) Stretch dough into a 12-inch round on a floured surface.
4) Spread sauce, top with mozzarella, and slide onto hot stone or sheet.
5) Bake 8-12 minutes until crust is blistered.
6) Add basil, drizzle olive oil, and slice to serve.
