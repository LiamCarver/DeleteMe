Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 8-10 fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 475 F with a pizza stone if available.
2. Stretch dough on a floured surface.
3. Spread sauce, add mozzarella, and drizzle olive oil.
4. Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
5. Top with basil and a pinch of salt.
