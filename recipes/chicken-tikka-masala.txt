Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lb chicken thighs, bite-size
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Salt

Steps:
1. Mix yogurt, spices, garlic, ginger, and salt; coat chicken and marinate 30 minutes.
2. Sear chicken in a hot skillet until browned; set aside.
3. Melt butter, saute onion until soft.
4. Add tomato sauce and simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and return chicken to the pan.
6. Simmer 10 minutes until cooked through.
7. Serve with rice or naan.
