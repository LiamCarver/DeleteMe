Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:
- 1 lb boneless chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (14 oz)
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt

Steps:
1. Mix yogurt, spices, lemon juice, ginger, garlic, and salt.
2. Marinate chicken 30 minutes or overnight.
3. Sear chicken in oil until browned; remove.
4. Saute onion until soft.
5. Add tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes.
6. Return chicken, add cream, simmer 10 minutes.
7. Serve with rice or naan.
