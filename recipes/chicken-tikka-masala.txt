Chicken Tikka Masala

Serves: 4
Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 lb boneless chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 2 tbsp oil or ghee
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- Cilantro, for serving

Steps:
1) Marinate chicken with yogurt, lemon, garam masala, cumin, paprika, and salt for 20 minutes.
2) Sear chicken in a hot skillet with 1 tbsp oil until browned; set aside.
3) Saute onion in remaining oil until soft. Add garlic, ginger, and tomato paste for 1 minute.
4) Stir in crushed tomatoes and sugar; simmer 10 minutes.
5) Add chicken and juices; simmer 8 minutes.
6) Stir in cream, warm through, and serve with cilantro and rice.
