Chicken Tikka Masala

Servings: 4
Time: 50 minutes

Ingredients
- 1.5 lb boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tbsp butter
- Fresh cilantro, to serve

Steps
1. Mix yogurt, garam masala, cumin, paprika, and salt. Coat chicken and marinate 20 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear chicken until browned, 4 to 5 minutes, then remove.
3. Add onion to the pan and cook until soft. Stir in garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.
4. Add tomato paste and crushed tomatoes. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and butter, then return chicken to the sauce. Simmer 8 to 10 minutes.
6. Serve with cilantro and rice or naan.
