Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:
- 600 g chicken thigh, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup cream
- Salt

Steps:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, 1 tsp garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika, and salt for 30 minutes.
2. Sear chicken in a hot pan until browned; remove.
3. Saute onion in oil until golden; add garlic and ginger for 1 minute.
4. Add remaining garam masala and tomatoes; simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and return chicken; cook 8 to 10 minutes until done.
6. Serve with rice or naan.
