Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tbsp tikka masala spice mix
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (14 oz)
- 1/2 cup cream
- Salt

Steps:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and 1 tbsp spice mix for 30 minutes.
2. Sear chicken in oil until browned; remove.
3. Saute onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
4. Stir in remaining spice mix and tomatoes; simmer 10 minutes.
5. Add chicken and cook through, 5-7 minutes.
6. Stir in cream and salt to taste. Serve with rice.
