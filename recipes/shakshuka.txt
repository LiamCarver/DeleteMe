Shakshuka

Serves: 3-4
Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 4-6 eggs
- Parsley, for serving

Steps:
1) Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onion and pepper until soft.
2) Add garlic and spices; cook 1 minute.
3) Stir in tomatoes, salt, and pepper; simmer 10 minutes.
4) Make wells and crack in eggs. Cover and cook until whites set.
5) Garnish with parsley and serve with bread.
