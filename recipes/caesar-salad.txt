Caesar Salad

Ingredients:
- 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1/2 cup croutons
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, mustard, garlic, and mayonnaise.
2. Season dressing with salt and pepper.
3. Toss lettuce with dressing.
4. Top with croutons and parmesan.
