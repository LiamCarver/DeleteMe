# Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu

## Ingredients
- 14 oz firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 carrot, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

## Steps
1. Pat tofu dry and sear in oil until golden; remove from pan.
2. Stir-fry vegetables for 3-4 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
4. Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and cornstarch slurry.
5. Return tofu to pan, pour in sauce, and toss until glossy.
6. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
