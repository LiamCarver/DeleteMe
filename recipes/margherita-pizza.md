# Margherita Pizza

## Ingredients
- 1 pizza dough
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 500 F with a pizza stone if available.
2. Stretch dough into a 12-inch round.
3. Spread tomato sauce over dough.
4. Top with mozzarella and a pinch of salt.
5. Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is browned.
6. Finish with basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
