Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients:
- 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Season shrimp with salt and pepper.
2. Melt butter with oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Add garlic for 30 seconds, then add shrimp.
4. Cook shrimp 2 minutes per side.
5. Add wine and lemon juice; simmer 1-2 minutes.
6. Sprinkle parsley and serve.
