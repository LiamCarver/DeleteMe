Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 475 F.
2. Stretch dough into a round and place on a baking sheet.
3. Spread tomato sauce over dough.
4. Add mozzarella slices and season with salt and pepper.
5. Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
6. Top with basil and drizzle olive oil before serving.
