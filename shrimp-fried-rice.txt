Shrimp Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked rice, chilled
- 8 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1/2 cup peas and carrots
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Salt and pepper

Steps:
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok; scramble eggs and remove.
2. Add remaining oil and cook shrimp until pink; remove.
3. Add peas and carrots, stir-fry 2 minutes.
4. Add rice and toss to heat through.
5. Stir in soy sauce, sesame oil, eggs, shrimp, and green onions.
6. Season to taste and serve.
