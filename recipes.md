# Recipes

## Lemon Garlic Pasta

Ingredients
- 8 oz spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 lemon, zested and juiced
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper

Steps
1. Cook spaghetti until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Warm olive oil, add garlic, and cook until just golden.
3. Toss pasta with garlic oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, and a splash of pasta water.
4. Stir in Parmesan and parsley; season with salt and pepper.

## Sheet Pan Chicken and Veg

Ingredients
- 4 chicken thighs
- 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper

Steps
1. Heat oven to 425F.
2. Toss potatoes, broccoli, and chicken with olive oil, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast on a sheet pan for 30 to 35 minutes, turning once, until chicken is cooked through.
