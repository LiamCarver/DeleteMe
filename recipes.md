# Recipes

## Lemon Herb Chicken
Ingredients:
- 2 boneless chicken breasts
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon (zest and juice)
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and black pepper

Steps:
1. Mix olive oil, lemon zest and juice, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
2. Coat chicken and marinate for 15 minutes.
3. Sear in a hot pan for 4-5 minutes per side, then cook through over medium heat.

## Tomato Basil Pasta
Ingredients:
- 8 oz pasta
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, torn
- Salt and black pepper

Steps:
1. Cook pasta to al dente and reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. Saute garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes, and cook until softened.
3. Toss pasta with sauce and a splash of pasta water; finish with basil and seasoning.
