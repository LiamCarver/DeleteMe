Lentil Soup Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon wedges (optional)

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a pot and saute onion, carrots, and celery until soft.
2. Add garlic and cumin; cook 30 seconds.
3. Add lentils, broth, and tomatoes. Bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat and simmer 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with lemon if desired.
