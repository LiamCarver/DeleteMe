Beef Tacos Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper
- Taco shells or tortillas
- Toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese, salsa

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a skillet and cook onion until soft.
2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
3. Add beef and cook until browned.
4. Stir in spices, salt, and pepper. Add a splash of water and simmer 3 minutes.
5. Fill shells with beef and add toppings.
6. Serve immediately.
