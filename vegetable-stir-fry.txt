Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 carrot, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a wok over high heat.
2. Add garlic and ginger; stir for 20 seconds.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes.
4. Add soy sauce and sesame oil.
5. Stir in cornstarch slurry and cook until glossy.
6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
