Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:
- 12 oz spaghetti
- 4 oz pancetta or bacon, diced
- 2 large eggs
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and black pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Chopped parsley (optional)

Steps:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook pancetta until crisp. Add garlic for 30 seconds.
3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan with a pinch of pepper.
4. Drain pasta and add to skillet off heat. Toss with pancetta.
5. Quickly stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed for a creamy sauce.
6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve.
