Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Salt

Steps:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, half the spices, and salt for 30 minutes.
2. Sear chicken in oil until browned; remove.
3. Saute onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
4. Add remaining spices, then tomatoes. Simmer 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream and return chicken. Simmer until cooked through.
6. Season to taste and serve with rice or naan.
